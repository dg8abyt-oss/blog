What Are You Paying For?

Why is there such a massive gap between the ₹30k freelancer and the ₹5 Lakh agency? It usually comes down to three things: Security, Scalability, and Speed.

The 2-Year Freelancer

Great for MVPs or personal projects. They will likely use templates and focus on "making it look right."

Risk: Backend security might be weak; code might be hard to update later.

The Senior Pro

They understand React Hooks, Node.js memory management, and database indexing. You aren't just paying for code; you're paying for architecture that won't crash when 1,000 users hit it at once.

The Agency

You get a team: A UI designer, a Frontend Dev, a Backend Dev, and a QA tester. The communication is professional, and they offer contracts with support guarantees.

Big Tech Firms

For large-scale enterprise apps requiring ISO certifications, heavy legal contracts, and custom server infrastructure. Overkill for most small businesses.
